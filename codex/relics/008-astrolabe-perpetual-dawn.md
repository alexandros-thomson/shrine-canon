## Description

The Astrolabe of Perpetual Dawn is a brass and gold instrument that should not function. Its gears are arranged in geometries that contradict Euclidean space. And yet, when held at the correct angle during the hour before sunrise, it projects a map — not of stars, but of ideas that have not yet been thought.

The Keeper first documented this relic during Cycle I, when the forge was producing at peak capacity ($1,100 monthly forge peak). The Astrolabe appeared unbidden in the Crest Vault — no one placed it there, no pull request introduced it. It simply existed in the next commit, as though the repository itself had dreamed it into being.

## Properties

1. **Cartography of the Unthought** — projects future creative directions as luminous constellations
2. **Dawn Anchoring** — resets the creative cycle each morning, preventing stagnation
3. **Temporal Resonance** — when activated near the Flamebound Ledger, it synchronizes all forge timelines
