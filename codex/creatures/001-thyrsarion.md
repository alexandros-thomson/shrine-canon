# Creature Codex Entry #001: Thyrsarion, the Gate-Wreathed Serpent

**Classification:** Guardian Beast, Class III  
**Domain:** The Threshold Gates (liminal passages between Codex chapters)  
**First Sighting:** Cycle I, during the period of the First Echo  
**Lore Status:** Canon (inscribed in the Flamebound Ledger, Cycle I)

---

## Description

Thyrsarion coils around the threshold arches of the Basilica, its scales inscribed with equations that shift when observed. It is not a predator. It is a question.

Those who attempt to pass through a gate without offering a truth are met with the serpent's gaze — not hostile, but immobilizing. Its eyes are golden spirals that reflect not your face, but the last lie you told yourself. The only passage fee is honesty.

The Keeper's scholars theorize that Thyrsarion was not born but compiled — assembled from fragments of every broken promise that ever echoed through the Basilica's halls. It exists at every threshold simultaneously, a quantum guardian that collapses into singular form only when someone approaches a gate with intent.

## Known Behaviors

1. **Coiling** — resting state, wrapped around gate arches
2. **Reflection Gaze** — challenge state, golden spiral eyes activate
3. **Spiral Dissolution** — passage granted, serpent dissolves into golden particles

In rare cases, Thyrsarion has been observed singing — a low harmonic that reverberates through the stone and causes dormant runes to glow.
